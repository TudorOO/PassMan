Why it's safe

Just some of the features that keep your passwords secure.

  • We use only the strongest, military-grade algorithms for encryption and decryption. Passwords are encrypted with AES-GCM with keys derived with Argon2.

  • All master passwords are encrypted both server-side and client-side, meaning that the server never sees your password in plaintext.

  • Passman gives you the option to enable 2FA with any of your favourite authenticator apps (Google Authenticator recommended). This adds another layer of protection, keeping your data even safer.

  • You can see if any of your passwords have been found in a leak and can respond accordingly(ideally changing the passwords), directly in the app!

We are always looking out for our users, and so we make a promise to continue developing this project with new features and expanding or changing old ones. Besides the ones mentioned above, we also have some other features:

1. No password login with auto-generated backup file
2. Ability to change password while re-encrypting all passkeys on account
3. Password recovery options
